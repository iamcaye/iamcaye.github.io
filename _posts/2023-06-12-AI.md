## CNN Arquitecture
# Typical CNN
It's made of two stages:
* **Stage 1:**
    It is a series of convolutions followed by pooling layers (feature transformer).
* **Stage 2:**
This one is made of dense layers. They are just some feed forward layers (non linear transformer).
